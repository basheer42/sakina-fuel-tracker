What is TR830?

TR830 is a KRA document that shows captured/received entries per product per destination. The system will automatically extract:

  • • Date: From the top right corner (import date)
  • • AVALUE: Quantity in litres
  • • MARKS: Vessel/shipment identifier
  • • DESCRIPTION: Product type (Gasoil=AGO, Mogas=PMS) and destination
